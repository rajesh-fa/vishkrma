Vishwakarma Puja

Vishwakarma Puja, also known as Vishwakarma Day, is celebrated every year on the 16th or 17th of September and the day after Diwali. On this day, Lord Vishwakarma, the creator of the entire world, is worshiped; according to the belief, Lord Vishwakarma alone has created the whole world. This Hindu festival is associated with him only.

According to the religious books, Lord Vishwakarma is known as Devashilpi or The Architect of Gods. He is also known as the sole architect and craftsman of the entire Trilok as well as the mortal region...
